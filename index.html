Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

HoopTrack ("we", "our", or "the app") is a basketball statistics tracker built for coaches and players. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. HoopTrack requires no account, no sign-in, and no registration of any kind. The app does not collect your name, email address, location, or any other identifying information.

All team rosters, game statistics, and possession data you enter into the app are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

How We Use Your Information

Because we collect no information, there is nothing to use, share, or sell. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

Third-Party Services

HoopTrack does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or authentication services. No data is shared with any third party.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in your device's local application storage. We have no access to this data at any time. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

HoopTrack does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
