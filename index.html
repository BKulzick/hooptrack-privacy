Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

HoopTrack ("we", "our", or "the app") is a basketball statistics tracker built for coaches and players. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google or Apple we receive:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • A unique account identifier from the provider

All team rosters, game statistics, and possession data you enter into the app are stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit or store this data on any server.

How We Use Your Information

Sign-in credentials are used solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity when opening the app
  • Display your name or email within the app

We do not sell, rent, share, or use your personal information for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

Third-Party Sign-In Services

HoopTrack uses Sign in with Google and Sign in with Apple for authentication. By using these services you are also subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Data Storage & Security

Game data and rosters are stored in your device's local application storage. We have no access to this data. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

HoopTrack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
